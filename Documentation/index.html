Shareious Image Sharing Documentation

Share you images in a simple and unique way with fancy animations. Drag or click the social buttons to share

How to install Shareious Plugin

After purchasing the plugin you need to install it. The first step is to head over to Plugins-> Add New

Add new

From the Plugins Menu click the Add New button

Upload Plugin

Click Upload Plugin and upload the .zip file named shareious.zip

Activate Plugin

After uploading the plugin click on Activate Plugin

First use

After activating this plugin head over to Shareious plugin page. This is how the first page looks like:

Shareious Image Sharing contains 3 tabs, each one with different settings.
  1. Social Networks - Here you can choose your prefered social networks.
  2. Social Identity - Here you can paste your Twitter username and your Facebook Application ID
  3. Advanced- Here are the Advanced Features as animations, sharing modes and settings for images
In order to use this plugin you need to have posts or custom posts with images.
The first step is to head over to Posts and click on New post or edit an existing one.
Click on Add media and choose your image(you can choose multiple images too):
You can also deactivate the plugin by switching ON the Deactivate Shareious? button.

Social Networks

Here you have the option to enable/disable certain social networks.

By default the first 4 social networks will be enabled when you first activate Shareious Image Sharing(Facebook, Google+, Twitter and Pinterest) .
To enable a social network click the ON/OFF button . When the button gets green the social network is activated and the user can share your image on that social platform. 
Please be aware, for proper use at least one social media network must be enabled.

Social Identity

In the social Social identity tab you can add your social credentials.

In the first field please paste your Facebook Application ID. If you don't have one please head over to this link. Example: 247670734547877

In the second field add your twitter username without the @ symbol.
Example: john_doe

Sharing modes

By default the first the sharing modes are disabled and only the basic click to share mode is activated.

Drag to share

This sharing mode let's you to share your image in an unique way. All you need to do is to hover over the image and drag down the social buttons that appear until a drop button appear.

You can try this feature on the image bellow:

Special share

This sharing mode allows you to share using only a button. On the right side of this section you can modify the color of that button using the Choose color.

To see how this share mode works hover over the image bellow and drag the button that appear and place it on one social icon button.

Ignore Images

This setting is useful if you want to deactivate certain Shareious Image Sharing for certain images.
This setting uses 2 input fields. The first field is used to specify what images to ignore based on their width. If you enter a value as 300 those images that are smaller in width than 300px will be ignored by Shareious Image Sharing.
The second field is used to specify what images to ignore based on their height. If you enter a value as 300 those images that are smaller in height than 300px will be ignored by Shareious Image Sharing.

Ignore images from post/pages or custom posts

The Deactivate Shareious on this post section will appear on all posts/pages or custom post. If you want to deactivate Shareious Image Sharing all you have to do is turning ON this setting. Once this setting is on Shareious Image Sharing will ignore all the related images.
